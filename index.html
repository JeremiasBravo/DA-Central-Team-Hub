The Central Backlog is the operational sub-product: the concrete actions, owners, deadlines, and RAG status that emerge from those priorities.

Automations & reminders
Deadline alerts · Status change notifications · Slack triggers
How automations connect to Slack
Each automation watches priorities and backlog items. When a trigger fires, it sends a Slack notification to the configured channel or person. To connect to real Slack: your IT team needs to create a Slack App with a Webhook URL and add it to this tool's configuration. Until then, all notifications are simulated in this prototype.
